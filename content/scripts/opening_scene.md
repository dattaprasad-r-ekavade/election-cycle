# OPENING SCENE: "The Bait-and-Switch"

## Overview

A frame-perfect Pokemon parody that gets violently interrupted by "Legal."
Designed as streamer bait - the whiplash should generate genuine reactions.

**Runtime:** ~90 seconds (unskippable first time, skippable on replay)

---

## TECHNICAL NOTES

- Use pixel art style matching classic Pokemon
- Music: Public domain "inspiring journey" style (NOT actual Pokemon music)
- All transitions should feel authentic until the interruption
- The interruption should have NO transition - hard cut, jarring

---

## SCENE BREAKDOWN

### BEAT 1: THE SETUP (0:00 - 0:25)

**[FADE FROM BLACK]**

**[A familiar scene: A cozy laboratory interior. Bookshelves. A desk.
Pixel art style. Everything screams "you know exactly what this is."]**

**[An elderly figure in a lab coat stands center frame.
He has white hair, kind eyes, and an unsettling smile.]**

**[Text box appears with classic RPG font]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Hello there! Welcome to the world of...
```

**[He pauses. Looks directly at camera. Holds eye contact too long.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
...POLITICS.
```

**[A small pixelated creature appears beside him.
It's a man in a suit. He waves. His smile is unsettling.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
This world is inhabited by creatures called...
POLITICIANS.
```

---

### BEAT 2: THE ESCALATION (0:25 - 0:50)

**[The suited creature does a little spin. Sparkles appear.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
For some people, POLITICIANS are pets.
They keep them in their pockets. Tiny,
obedient, bought and paid for.
```

**[Professor Elmwood's expression hasn't changed. Still smiling.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Others use POLITICIANS for fighting.
They send them into arenas to battle for
dominance, reputation, and...
```

**[Dramatic pause]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
...campaign contributions.
```

**[The politician creature opens a briefcase. Money flies out.
The professor nods approvingly.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
As for myself...
```

**[He gestures grandly]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I study POLITICIANS as a profession.
Their habitats. Their behaviors.
Their elaborate mating rituals.
```

---

### BEAT 3: THE POINT OF NO RETURN (0:50 - 1:00)

**[Professor zooms in slightly. His smile widens.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
But first, tell me a little about yourself.
```

**[Two silhouettes appear - one masculine, one feminine,
classic character selection style]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Now then... are you a boy? Or are you a—
```

---

### BEAT 4: THE INTERRUPTION (1:00 - 1:15)

**[HARD CUT. No transition. Screen tears slightly.]**

**[A DIFFERENT PERSON bursts into frame from the right side.
They're wearing a modern business suit. They're sweating.
They have a lanyard that says "LEGAL."]**

**[The pixel art style GLITCHES for a moment -
we see a flash of higher resolution panic]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
STOP. STOP STOP STOP STOP STOP.
```

**[Professor Elmwood freezes mid-gesture.
His smile is still frozen on his face.]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
We can't do this. We talked about this.
This is... this is EXACTLY what they said
we couldn't do.
```

**[Professor Elmwood's frozen smile twitches.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I don't understand. I was simply introducing
the player to the world of—
```

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I KNOW WHAT YOU WERE DOING.
EVERYONE knows what you were doing.
```

---

### BEAT 5: THE AWKWARD AFTERMATH (1:15 - 1:35)

**[Long pause. The politician creature is still on screen,
frozen mid-wave, money still floating in the air.]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Just... just start the actual game.
```

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
But I haven't finished my—
```

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE ACTUAL. GAME.
```

**[Professor Elmwood deflates. His sprite visibly sags.]**

```
PROFESSOR ELMWOOD
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
...Fine.
```

**[He shuffles off screen left.
The Legal Person watches him go.]**

**[The politician creature is STILL on screen.
Still frozen. Still holding money.]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
You too.
```

**[The politician creature's frozen smile falters.
It slowly moonwalks off screen.]**

---

### BEAT 6: THE RECOVERY (1:35 - 1:50)

**[The Legal Person faces the camera.
They try to compose themselves.
They're not doing great.]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Okay. Hi. Sorry about that.
```

**[They straighten their lanyard]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Welcome to ELECTION CYCLE.
A completely original game about politics.
That we made. Ourselves.
```

**[Beat]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
You're going to run for local office.
You have seven days to win an election.
Everything you do has consequences.
```

**[They glance off-screen nervously, as if checking
if the professor is still watching]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
There are no... "creatures."
No "catching" anything.
You just lie to people and hope for the best.
```

**[They pause]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
...Like real politics.
```

---

### BEAT 7: THE TRANSITION (1:50 - END)

**[They gesture vaguely toward the camera]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
So. What's your name?

Not your TRAINER name. Not your...
Just your name. For the campaign.
```

**[The screen begins to transition to the actual character creation screen]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Good luck out there.
Try not to get sued.
```

**[They mutter as they walk off]**

```
LEGAL PERSON
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
(At least one of us should manage that...)
```

**[TRANSITION TO: Character Creation Screen]**

## POST-SCENE NOTES

### On Replay
- Show "SKIP INTRO?" prompt after 2 seconds
- If skipped, hard cut to character creation with quick text:
  ```
  LEGAL PERSON
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
  You again? Fine. Name. Now.
  ```

### Easter Eggs (Optional)
- If player names themselves "Ash" or "Red":
  Legal Person appears briefly: "Nice try."
- If player names themselves "Professor":
  Legal Person: "Absolutely not."
- If player names themselves "Pikachu" or any Pokemon name:
  Screen flickers, Legal Person appears: "I'm watching you."

### Streamer Considerations
- The interruption timing is critical - it must feel like a mistake
- Professor Elmwood's frozen smile during the interruption is key
- The moonwalking politician is the "clip moment"
- Legal Person's increasing panic should escalate naturally
- The "try not to get sued" line is the final punchline

---

## DIALOGUE TONE GUIDE

**Professor Elmwood:**
- Warm, grandfatherly, slightly uncanny
- Speaks in familiar rhythms (you know the ones)
- Never breaks character even during interruption

**Legal Person:**
- Panicked millennial energy
- Speaks too fast, over-explains
- Genuinely stressed, not played for camp
- Their sincerity makes it funnier

**Politician Creature:**
- Never speaks
- Movement should be slightly wrong (uncanny valley)
- The moonwalk should be awkward, not smooth

---

## MUSIC/SOUND NOTES

- Opening: Gentle, adventurous (legally distinct)
- During professor speech: Builds warmth
- AT INTERRUPTION: Music cuts DEAD. Silence.
- Awkward aftermath: Maybe a single held note, uncomfortable
- Legal Person section: No music, just ambient
- Transition to game: Actual game music fades in

The silence at the interruption is crucial. It should feel like something broke.
