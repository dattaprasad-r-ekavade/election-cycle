10-SCENARIO STORY MODE — “THE UNLIKELY MAYOR TOUR”
Each scenario is a new town, new opponent, new culture, new chaos, but part of one coherent rise-to-power arc.

⭐ SCENARIO 1 — HOMETOWN: “Mom Said Run”
A tiny, sleepy town.
You’re jobless.
Mom volunteers you for mayor.
Opponent is an 80-year-old incumbent who’s forgotten he’s running.
This is the “origin story.”

⭐ SCENARIO 2 — COLLEGE TOWN: “Campus of Chaos”
You move to a vibrant, hyper-political university town.
Students protest everything (including their last protest).
Professors form factions.
The local frat runs an “unaccredited polling agency.”
Opponent: a Postmodern Philosophy Professor who only speaks in metaphors.
Theme: ideological storms, meme culture, frantic youth politics.

⭐ SCENARIO 3 — SUBURBAN TOWN: “HOA Wars”
A tidy middle-class town that is secretly an emotional warzone.
Five Homeowner Associations control everything.
People are polite yet unhinged.
Lawn rules stricter than aviation safety codes.
Opponent: a charismatic HOA Chair who campaigns door-to-door with a clipboard.
Theme: passive-aggressive politics, rules, order, aesthetic warfare.

⭐ SCENARIO 4 — RUSTBELT TOWN: “Industry of Problems”
A once-great industrial town on the decline.
Abandoned factories everywhere.
Strange smoke from the power plant.
Everything is unionized — even the vending machines union.
Opponent: a former factory foreman, beloved but stubborn.
Theme: working-class politics, grit, nostalgia, industrial satire.

⭐ SCENARIO 5 — TOURIST RETREAT: “Vacation Nightmare”
A resort town overflowing with seasonal chaos.
Tourists behave feral.
Locals resent tourists.
Gift shops wage silent economic wars.
Opponent: a celebrity travel vlogger who live-streams everything.
Theme: tourism economics, influencer culture, image management.

⭐ SCENARIO 6 — TECHNOLOGY TOWN: “Code, Drones & Drama”
You arrive in a futuristic startup-owned municipality.
Public services replaced by glitchy apps.
Drones deliver insults when malfunctioning.
Crypto-mines under city hall.
Opponent: a 26-year-old tech CEO who debates using AI deepfakes.
Theme: innovation satire, techno-utopianism, digital disorder.

⭐ SCENARIO 7 — DREAM SEQUENCE: “Welcome to Naptropolis”
A surreal, subconscious town you visit during a campaign burnout-induced nap.
Buildings built from past scenarios.
Opponents blended into monstrous hybrids.
Your SKILL stats manifest as characters.
Opponent: your own subconscious, with maxed-out stats you lack.
Theme: self-doubt, hallucinated politics, comedic psychodrama.
Purpose:
Emotional midpoint.
Reveals your weaknesses.
Sets ambition for final arc.

⭐ SCENARIO 8 — CRIME CITY: “Mayhem District”
You awaken in a gritty urban jungle after the dream.
Three factions control neighborhoods.
Elections are legal but confusingly intense.
Sirens score your speeches.
Opponent: a smooth-talking ex-lawyer with a suspiciously clean record.
Theme: noir satire, faction diplomacy, chaos management.

⭐ SCENARIO 9 — NATIONAL TEST TOWN: “Trial of the Titans”
A prestigious town where up-and-coming political stars compete.
It’s basically a political tournament arc disguised as a mayoral election.
Media everywhere.
Celebrities drop endorsements suddenly.
Scandals explode instantly on SKILL media.
Opponent: a TV personality who transitioned into politics yesterday.
Theme: national spotlight, media storms, image warfare.
This is your “proof you can go national” moment.

⭐ SCENARIO 10 — CAPITAL CITY: “The Final Mayor”
The grand finale.
A fictionalized U.S. capital (to avoid real politics): “Bureaucratia” or “Columbia Heights-District” or “Union City”.
Huge districts
Competing lobbyists
Complex voter blocs
Monument protests
Government shutdown threats
Every opponent from the campaign watching
Opponent:
A procedurally generated “super candidate” built as the perfect counter to your SKILL build.
Theme: everything coming together — ambition, chaos, satire, destiny.
Ending variations depend on:
Scandals
Alignment (idealistic, opportunistic, chaotic, wholesome)
SKILL stat profile
Whether your mom still calls after every victory
Number of frogs rained on your rallies
